Cookies:

When you visit this site, cookies will be placed on your machine for tracking visitor activity anonymously; targeting advertisements by interest, and remembering user preferences.

We set a cookie to remember that you don’t want to see the banner at the top of the page that warns you about cookies on this site. If you click the “That’s fine” button, you will not see this banner on future visits* and you will opt in to having a cookie placed on your machine by me.

This site makes use of some third party services who also store cookies (Third party cookies) on your machine. These services are

  • Google Analytics
  • Google Adsense

Note: cookies are browser-specific. If you visit using a different browser or on another machine, you may see the banner again.
